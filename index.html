Assessment of Food Expenditure Among Syrian Refugee Households

Business Problem

The Syrian crisis, which began in 2011, displaced over 5.5 million refugees, severely impacting their ability to meet basic food needs.

Factors like low income, large household size, and limited government aid affected food spending and health. This project analyzes the impact of these factors on household food spending in Kurdistan, Iraq, and Jordan.

Business Impact

  • Host Country Support: Guide food assistance programs for vulnerable households.
  • Organizational Planning: Help organizations design effective training and job programs.

Tools Used

  • Excel: Data cleaning & organization.
  • Python: Analysis using Pandas, NumPy, and Matplotlib.
  • Tableau: Dashboard for insights.
