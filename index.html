Assessment of Food Expenditure Among Syrian Refugees Households . 2025

The Syrian crisis, which began in 2011, resulted in the displacement of approximately 5.5 million refugees, severely affecting households' ability to meet their food needs. Food is considered one of the essential pillars for households, and factors such as low income, large household sizes, and limited government assistance have made it difficult to cover food expenditure, influencing food choices and overall health. Will analyze these factors to assess their impact on food expenditure patterns and evaluate the overall effect in households in Kurdistan/Iraq and Jordan.
